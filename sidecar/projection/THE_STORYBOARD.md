# THE STORYBOARD вҖ” the surface, scene by scene, across every verb

**The missing middle.** `THE_VOICE.md` is the *register* (how the instrument speaks);
`THE_VOICE_INTEGRATION.md` is the *build* (how the copy becomes structural). This document
is what sits between them: the **shot list** вҖ” the major stages of every run, the streams of
information that enter and exit each frame, the subjects that take the stage, and the
positive / negative / edge outcomes each can land on вҖ” storyboarded the way a producer blocks
a scene, so that *only then* the diction can be inventoried against real surfaces rather than
guessed.

It is grounded in two canonical surfaces, cited throughout:
- **`THE_USE_CASE_ONTOLOGY.md`** вҖ” the amino-acid alphabet (В§2, the *cast*), the protein catalog
  (В§3, the *scene sequences*), the master matrix (В§4, the *streams per subject*), the laws (В§5,
  the *counts and spectrums*).
- **`THE_VOICE.md`** вҖ” the verdict set (В§3), the move copy (В§4), the gates (В§5), the proofs (В§6),
  the scale discipline (В§12), Watch (В§13), setup (В§14).

Copy here is **illustrative**, per `THE_VOICE.md`'s anchor discipline вҖ” read for the stream and
the register, not the exact string. When a surface lands, its copy is *derived* from `THE_VOICE.md`,
its streams from this storyboard, its proof from the ontology cell.

---

## 0 вҖ” How to read this

A film is **acts** (the arc), **a cast** (who can appear), **streams of light and sound** (what the
camera captures), and **a call sheet per scene** (who's actually on stage that day). This storyboard
is the same four things:

1. **The spine (В§1)** вҖ” the nine acts every run moves through. The universal arc.
2. **The six streams (В§2)** вҖ” the taxonomy of *what kind of information* can flow in any act:
   counts В· spectrums В· forecasts В· signals В· interventions В· intentions.
3. **The shot list (В§3)** вҖ” the heart. Per act: the moment, who enters and exits, the streams in
   frame, the intervention (if the act can stop), and the **positive / negative / edge** outcomes вҖ”
   each given equal design care.
4. **The call sheets (В§4)** вҖ” the nine verbs (proteins P-1вҖҰP-9) cast against the acts: which subjects
   take the stage, the felt arc, the one line each leads with.

Then, and only then:

5. **The diction inventory (В§5)** вҖ” the vocabulary, bound to each stream-type and each outcome
   register. This is where the voice becomes specific.

**The Apple-quality claim, stated up front.** Quality is not the polish of the happy path. It is that
the **negative and the edge get the identical care as the positive** вҖ” the refusal is as calm and
as concrete as the success; the no-op is a designed moment, not an empty screen; the failure hands
over a move instead of a stack trace; and the surface is *the same size* whether three things changed
or three thousand (`THE_VOICE.md` В§12). The storyboard exists to make that symmetry checkable: every
act below carries all three outcomes, or it isn't done.

## 1 вҖ” The spine: the nine acts

Every protein in the ontology is the same rhythm вҖ”
`Snapshot вҶ’ Diff вҶ’ Gate вҶ’ realize вҶ’ Measure вҶ’ Verify вҶ’ Record` (ontology В§3; `THE_VOICE.md` В§7) вҖ”
bracketed by an establishing shot (setup) and a closing shot (the timeline). Stage names are **what
they do for the operator**, never the engine verb (`THE_VOICE.md` В§13).

| Act | Operator-facing name | Engine verb | The moment (felt sense) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **0** | **Arrival** | (config / pre-flight) | *what's set, what isn't, can I reach it* вҖ” the establishing shot |
| **1** | **Reading** | Snapshot | *here's what you have* (and *what's deployed*) вҖ” orientation |
| **2** | **What changed** | Diff | *here's the difference, big rocks first* вҖ” recognition |
| **3** | **The one decision** | Gate | *I'm stopping to ask* вҖ” consent (the intervention act) |
| **4** | **Making it real** | realize (the moves) | *the changes happen, live* вҖ” motion (Watch) |
| **5** | **The touch** | Measure | *I touched exactly this much* вҖ” minimality, proven |
| **6** | **The self-check** | Verify | *I checked it matches* вҖ” fidelity, proven |
| **7** | **Recorded** | Record | *this run is saved* вҖ” continuity |
| **8** | **Where you are** | (timeline + ladder) | *the run takes its place; here's the distance* вҖ” the arc |

Three things to hold:

- **Acts are elided, never reordered.** A pure read (`diff`) plays acts 0вҖ“2 and stops. An idempotent
  redeploy (P-5) plays every act but acts 3вҖ“5 are *near-silent by design* (nothing to consent, nothing
  to touch). Drift (P-8) replaces act 4 with a decision (accept / remediate / escalate). The arc is a
  partial order (ontology В§3 cross-protein constraints), and the surface honors it.
- **Act 3 is the only act that stops the world.** Everything else streams; the gate *waits*. That
  asymmetry is the whole safety story, so it gets its own act even though it's often empty.
- **Acts 5вҖ“6 are the proofs.** They are where the engine's deepest claim about itself вҖ” *it is
  faithful* вҖ” is said to everyone as plain confidence, with the formal proof one dig down
  (`THE_VOICE.md` В§6). They are the soul of the surface, not a footer.

## 2 вҖ” The six streams (the taxonomy of what flows)

Everything the surface can show in any act is one of six kinds. Naming them is what lets us inventory
the diction precisely (В§5): each stream-type has its own rendering law.

### 2.1 Counts вҖ” scalar magnitudes
*What.* `N tables`, `N rows`, `вҖ–ОҙвҖ–` (total moves), CDC capture count, `N green checks`, `N orphans`,
`N runs`, churn-vs-net (`ChangeManifest.pathLength` vs net displacement; ontology В§4.4).
*Rendering law (`THE_VOICE.md` В§12).* Humane numerals (`2,140`, not `2140`); named units (`4,210 rows`);
**big rocks first**, impact-ranked never alphabetical; **cap-and-name** the tail (`and 1,847 more`,
with a way to reach them, never a scroll). The number scales; the sentence does not.

### 2.2 Spectrums вҖ” a graded position on a named scale
*What.* Faithfulness *class* (the 3-band: `faithful` / `lossy-with-warning` / `refuse-unless-declared`;
ontology В§4.1). Faithfulness *ladder* (`L1 witness вҶ’ L2 faithful вҶ’ L3 composed`; ontology В§5.3) вҖ”
rendered to the operator as **readiness** (`7 of 10 green`). The cutover streak. A drift's severity
(intended vs unintended). A reshape's instant-vs-rewrite band.
*Rendering law (`THE_VOICE.md` В§6, В§8).* **Never the L1/L2/L3 jargon on the lead.** A spectrum is shown
as a *distance* (`3 green to go` вҖ” the most motivating framing) and as **a single lever** (`the one
thing in the way: 3 accounts to map`), never a list of ten problems. The notation (`schema вң“вң“вң“ В·
identity вң“вң“в–І`) lives one dig down.

### 2.3 Forecasts вҖ” a predictive claim made *before* the act runs
*What.* The ETA (`~8s left`). The pre-flight reads: `4 rows are longer than 20 chars today` (data-compat,
ontology В§4.6); `this will rebuild the table` vs `instant` (widen vs narrow, ontology В§4.2 Reshape);
`3 of 142 users don't match UAT yet` (validate-user-map); `would throw` translated before it throws
(`THE_VOICE.md` В§14). The DacFx plan preview (the ALTER-lens; ontology В§4.5).
*Rendering law (`THE_VOICE.md` В§4, В§13, В§14).* State the **consequence as meaning, before it happens**
(`Narrowing Status (50 вҶ’ 20). I'll check your data first.`). Name the instant-vs-rewrite distinction
honestly. The ETA **degrades calmly** вҖ” when it can't estimate, it shows *none*; never a progress bar
that lies.

### 2.4 Signals вҖ” a binary or small-state indicator
*What.* The verdict (the closed set of 7; `THE_VOICE.md` В§3). Green / red. CDC-silent (the green hush).
Drift present / absent. Connection live / unreachable. The idempotent no-op. `it matches`.
*Rendering law (`THE_VOICE.md` В§3).* One of seven verdict lines, one glyph (`вң“ в–І вң•`), level tone вҖ” the
most dangerous thing on screen stated most calmly. Define by what *is* (`no removals`), never by what
isn't (`nothing destroyed`). Idempotence as a *conclusory* remark, never the lead
(`Nothing changed вҖ” and that's real.`).

### 2.5 Interventions вҖ” the act where the instrument stops and hands over a lever
*What.* The gates (`THE_VOICE.md` В§5; ontology В§4.6): declared-loss В· validate-user-map В·
declare-every-drop В· intent-filter В· data-compat В· provenance-completeness В· drift В· self-check вҖ”
plus the pre-flight gates that fire at arrival (connection В· permission В· CDC-tracking).
*Rendering law (`THE_VOICE.md` В§5).* Say the danger **as meaning**; name the **one** lever; hand over a
**plain active verb** (Approve В· Map В· Confirm В· Trim В· Record В· Accept В· Remediate В· Stop); then
*wait*. Never a wall of error, never `Proceed (Y/n)`, never `Override`.

### 2.6 Intentions вҖ” the communication register the act is trying to produce
*What.* The felt arc (`THE_VOICE.md` В§7): reassure В· orient В· warn В· ask-consent В· hand-off В· prove В·
motivate. This is the cinematographer's intent вҖ” the emotional shape the operator moves through.
*Rendering law (`THE_VOICE.md` В§1).* Calm by default; one surface, two velocities (newcomer and master,
same words); end on the move; *it's yours*. The intention is carried by tone and by what we choose to
surface, not by an explicit label.

> **The mapping that makes this useful:** every cell in the shot list (В§3) is a small set of these six
> streams, in a known register, with a known rendering law. So "design the surface for act N of verb V"
> becomes "render *these* counts, *this* spectrum, *this* forecast, under *this* intention" вҖ” and the
> diction (В§5) is the lookup from stream-type to words.

## 3 вҖ” The shot list (per act: the storyboard proper)

Each act is a card with the same fields: **the moment В· camera intent В· who enters/exits В· streams in
frame В· the intervention В· positive / negative / edge В· diction**. The three outcomes are the heart вҖ”
each gets a verdict line and a lead.

---

### ACT 0 вҖ” Arrival *(setup / pre-flight)*

- **Moment.** Before anything runs: what's configured, what isn't, can I reach the target.
- **Camera intent.** *Orient, never scold.* A thing that isn't set is **a choice to make, not a
  failure** (`THE_VOICE.md` В§14).
- **Who enters.** No moves yet. The cross-cutting verbs `Gate` (connection В· permission В· CDC-tracking
  pre-flights, ontology В§4.6) and the config surface.
- **Streams in frame.** *Signals* (connection live/not; history on/off). *Forecasts* (a required thing
  missing вҶ’ the clear ask). *Counts* (none).
- **Intervention.** The pre-flight gates: connection, permission. These refuse *first*, before any
  write (ontology В§4.6 / P-GATE).
- **Outcomes.**
  - **Positive** вҖ” `вң“` everything needed is set. Often *invisible* (we don't announce a clean arrival;
    we just proceed). On request: `History: on (./runs) В· Connection: not set В· Output: ./out`.
  - **Negative** вҖ” required + missing вҶ’ a clear ask: `I need a connection to UAT for this. Pass --conn
    <ref> (or set PROJECTION_CONN).` Set-but-invalid вҶ’ concrete + located: `line 12, "threshold" should
    be a number, not "ten". Fix it and rerun.` The variable name and stack live in the dig.
  - **Edge** вҖ” optional + unset вҶ’ an *invitation*, not red: `No history yet. Set PROJECTION_LEDGER_DIR
    to track your runs over time.` A would-throw caught and translated, never a raw exception.
- **Diction.** *Streams 2.3 (forecast) + 2.4 (signal).* The principle: the instrument never blames the
  operator for not knowing its switches (`THE_VOICE.md` В§14).

---

### ACT 1 вҖ” Reading *(Snapshot)*

- **Moment.** *Here's what you have.* The model read into a comparable value (and, for in-place/drift,
  *here's what's deployed*).
- **Camera intent.** *Quiet orientation.* The establishing wide shot of the estate.
- **Who enters.** `Snapshot` (ontology В§2.2 / В§4.3). For in-place and drift, a *second* Snapshot
  (the server) enters вҖ” the only act that can read two subjects.
- **Streams in frame.** *Counts* (`N tables`, `N columns`, rows profiled). *Signals* (read clean).
  In Watch, this is the first live stage: `Reading your model вң“ 1.2s`.
- **Intervention.** None (read-only).
- **Outcomes.**
  - **Positive** вҖ” `вң“` `Read your model вҖ” 300 tables.` (Often folded into the next act's lead.)
  - **Negative** вҖ” can't read: `That model won't load вҖ” line 12 is missing a name. Fix it and rerun.`
    / `Can't reach UAT. Check the connection and try again.` (`THE_VOICE.md` В§10.)
  - **Edge** вҖ” reads clean but the *profile is empty* (no data evidence): proceed, and let downstream
    acts say plainly what they couldn't check (honest-to-a-fault, `THE_VOICE.md` В§1.7). Partial read вҶ’
    name what's missing.
- **Diction.** *Stream 2.1 (count) + 2.4 (signal).* Concrete names from the start (`Country`, never
  `OS_KIND_Country`; `THE_VOICE.md` В§2.1, В§5).

---

### ACT 2 вҖ” What changed *(Diff)*

- **Moment.** *Here's the difference.* The displacement `B вҠ– A`, surfaced big-rocks-first.
- **Camera intent.** *Recognition without overwhelm.* The operator should see the few that matter and
  trust the rest is rolled up honestly.
- **Who enters.** The schema moves on stage as *named subjects*: **Add В· Remove В· Rename В· Reshape**
  (ontology В§4.2); on the data plane, **Insert В· Update В· Delete В· Unchanged** (В§4.3). Each move is
  rendered essence-first (`THE_VOICE.md` В§4). `Tolerate` enters quietly вҖ” the cosmetic differences
  named and set aside.
- **Streams in frame.** *Counts* (`вҖ–ОҙвҖ–`, per-channel move counts, `N removals`, `N narrowings`).
  *Spectrums* (each change's faithfulness class вҖ” safe / check-first / needs-consent). *Forecasts*
  (`Made Email longer (50 вҶ’ 100). Instant.` / `Narrowing Status вҖ” I'll check your data first.`).
  *Signals* (the verdict line).
- **Intervention.** None *here* вҖ” but a detected Remove / narrowing / drop *routes forward to Act 3*.
  The diff names it; the gate asks it.
- **Outcomes.**
  - **Positive** вҖ” `вң“ Safe to ship.` `14 changes В· all additive В· review before applying.` The big
    rocks named, the cosmetic rolled up: `2,124 safe additions В· 380 cosmetic, ignored on purpose.`
  - **Negative** вҖ” a destructive move is present: `в–І Paused вҖ” needs your okay.` `Removes the
    IX_Order_Stale index. Nothing else is lost.` (Hands to Act 3.) This is not an error; it's a
    recognition that routes to consent.
  - **Edge** вҖ” **no changes at all**: `вң“ No changes.` (The diff is empty over every declared facet;
    ontology В§5.0 P-CMP.) **At scale**: `2,140 changes across 300 tables В· 4 removals В· review before
    applying.` вҖ” same one-line verdict; cluster by table on the dig (`Sales В· 412 changes в–ё`); find,
    don't scroll (`THE_VOICE.md` В§12).
- **Diction.** *Streams 2.1 + 2.2 + 2.3.* The move lexicon (`THE_VOICE.md` В§4); the scale discipline
  (В§12). Never `Оҙ`, `norm`, `OS_KIND_*` on the lead (В§2.2).

---

### ACT 3 вҖ” The one decision *(Gate вҖ” the intervention act)*

- **Moment.** *I'm stopping to ask before I write.* The single act that waits.
- **Camera intent.** *Consent, not alarm.* A question a person can answer вҖ” never a wall of error.
- **Who enters.** `Declare(-loss)` / `Gate` (ontology В§2.2, В§4.6). Exactly **one lever** takes the
  stage at a time, even when several gates could fire (`THE_VOICE.md` В§5, В§8 вҖ” "never a list of ten").
- **Streams in frame.** *Interventions* (the gate copy + the active verb). *Forecasts* (the danger as
  meaning: `4 rows are longer than that today`). *Counts* (the precise scope: `3 of 142 users`).
- **Intervention.** This *is* the intervention. The eight operator-facing gates (`THE_VOICE.md` В§5):
  declared-loss В· validate-user-map В· declare-every-drop В· intent-filter В· data-compat В·
  provenance-completeness В· drift В· self-check. Each: *danger as meaning В· one lever В· active verb В·
  wait.*
- **Outcomes.**
  - **Positive** вҖ” **the act is empty**, and that is a designed outcome, not a missing screen: nothing
    to consent вҶ’ we *skip silently* to Act 4. (Most idempotent and additive runs.)
  - **Negative** вҖ” a gate fires: `в–І This removes the IX_Order_Stale index. No data is lost, but it
    can't be auto-undone.` вҶ’ button: **Approve what's removed, or stop.** The verb is always plain and
    active (Approve В· Map В· Confirm В· Trim В· Record В· Accept В· Remediate В· Stop).
  - **Edge** вҖ” *multiple* gates could fire вҶ’ we name the **most honest, least overwhelming** single
    lever and hold the rest beneath (`THE_VOICE.md` В§8). A drift gate hands a *three-way* lever (accept
    / remediate / escalate) вҖ” still one question, three plain answers.
- **Diction.** *Stream 2.5 (intervention).* The verb-on-the-button lexicon (`THE_VOICE.md` В§5);
  never `Declare loss`, never `Override`, never `Proceed (Y/N)`.

### ACT 4 вҖ” Making it real *(realize вҖ” Watch)*

- **Moment.** *The changes happen, live.* Stages fill in, in plain words, with a calm ETA.
- **Camera intent.** *Motion the operator can trust.* Progress that never lies; a no-op that reads as
  intentional.
- **Who enters.** The realizing moves: **Rename вҶ’ Reshape вҶ’ Add** (schema, in order), then **Move /
  Insert / Update / Unchanged / Delete** (data, FK-topological); `Reidentify` when crossing substrates
  (ontology В§3 ordering; В§4.4). The cross-cutting `Publish` hands the artifact to its terminus.
- **Streams in frame.** *Forecasts* (the ETA, `~8s left`; `142 of 300 tables`). *Counts* (rows moved,
  statements run, batches). *Signals* (per-stage `вң“ / вЈ· / в—Ӣ`). Live stage list (`THE_VOICE.md` В§13).
- **Intervention.** None mid-stream by design вҖ” the gate already fired (Act 3). The exception is a
  *mid-run failure*, which routes to a kind error (below).
- **Outcomes.**
  - **Positive** вҖ” stages land green with honest timings:
    `Reading your model вң“ 1.2s В· Checking the data вЈ· 142 of 300 В· ~8s left`. Each finished stage
    *points forward* (`THE_VOICE.md` В§13 вҖ” "nobody is left at done").
  - **Negative** вҖ” a stage fails mid-run (connection drops, permission denied, mid-statement abort):
    `вң• Stopped вҖ” here's what went wrong.` `I don't have permission to alter dbo.Order. Ask for ALTER,
    then retry.` And the safety promise: the sink is left **clean or at a deterministic resume point**
    (ontology В§4.6 P-EXE) вҖ” said plainly: `Nothing was half-written; safe to retry.`
  - **Edge** вҖ” **the no-op**: nothing to do вҶ’ the act is a single calm line, not an empty progress bar
    (`Nothing to do вҖ” and that's real.`). **ETA unknowable** вҶ’ show none, never a lying bar. **Resume**
    вҶ’ `Picking up where it stopped вҖ” rows 1вҖ“4,000 already loaded.`
- **Diction.** *Streams 2.3 (forecast/ETA) + 2.1 (count) + 2.4 (signal).* Stage names are operator-shaped
  (`Building the changes`, never `emit`); errors per `THE_VOICE.md` В§10.

### ACT 5 вҖ” The touch *(Measure вҖ” minimality proven)*

- **Moment.** *I touched exactly this much вҖ” nothing more.* The norm `вҖ–ОҙвҖ–`, read off the CDC ruler.
- **Camera intent.** *Quiet rigor.* The smallness of the touch is a kindness, said plainly, proven on
  the dig.
- **Who enters.** `Measure` (ontology В§2.2, В§5.6). The minimality proof (`THE_VOICE.md` В§6).
- **Streams in frame.** *Counts* (CDC capture count = `|true delta|`; per-channel `вҖ–ПҖ_c ОҙвҖ–`; churn vs
  net). *Spectrums* (isometric vs norm-inflating вҖ” minimal touch vs fresh-reload fallback). *Signals*
  (the green hush: `CDC = 0`).
- **Intervention.** None.
- **Outcomes.**
  - **Positive** вҖ” `вң“ I touched exactly the 312 rows that changed вҖ” nothing more.`
    dig: `вҖ–ОҙвҖ– = 312 = CDC capture count` (`THE_VOICE.md` В§6 minimality).
  - **Negative** вҖ” the honest fallback: a fresh wipe-and-load moved `2Г—|table|` rows. We **name it as
    the fallback, not hide it**: `Reloaded everything (the safe path) вҖ” more rows touched than strictly
    changed.` (ontology В§5.6 вҖ” norm-inflating is *why* it's the fallback.)
  - **Edge** вҖ” **CDC = 0**: the silence *is* the proof вҖ” `Nothing changed вҖ” and I made sure that's
    real, not a missed check.` (`THE_VOICE.md` В§6 CDC-silence.) **Couldn't measure** (CDC not tracked):
    say so вҖ” `Ran, but this table isn't CDC-tracked, so I can't prove the row count. Here's what I
    expect.`
- **Diction.** *Streams 2.1 (count) + 2.2 (spectrum) + 2.4 (signal).* `вҖ–ОҙвҖ–` and `capture count` live in
  the dig; the lead is `rows changed` / `rows touched` (`THE_VOICE.md` В§2.1).

### ACT 6 вҖ” The self-check *(Verify вҖ” fidelity proven)*

- **Moment.** *I checked it matches.* Read the result back, assert `Ingest вҲҳ Project = id` modulo named
  tolerances.
- **Camera intent.** *Earned confidence.* The deepest thing the engine knows about itself, said to
  everyone as plain trust, verifiable on the dig (`THE_VOICE.md` В§6).
- **Who enters.** `Verify` / the canary (ontology В§2.2, В§4.6, В§5.7). `Tolerate` (the named cosmetic
  residual).
- **Streams in frame.** *Signals* (`it matches` / `1 difference`). *Spectrums* (the ladder вҶ’ readiness).
  *Counts* (residual size; named tolerances).
- **Intervention.** A *failed* self-check fires the self-check gate (`THE_VOICE.md` В§5): the red blocks
  the commit.
- **Outcomes.**
  - **Positive** вҖ” `вң“ Done вҖ” and I checked: your database now matches your model.`
    dig: `residual вҲ… В· Ingest вҲҳ Project = id` (`THE_VOICE.md` В§6 commuting square).
  - **Negative** вҖ” `вң• My round-trip came back with 1 difference. Here it is вҖ” this needs a look before
    you ship.` (The one difference shown concretely; this blocks the commit.)
  - **Edge** вҖ” **ran but couldn't read back**: `It ran, but I couldn't read it back to confirm. Re-run
    the check when the server's reachable.` (`THE_VOICE.md` В§10 вҖ” honest about what it couldn't check.)
    **Equal-modulo-tolerance**: `Matches вҖ” with 3 cosmetic differences I'm ignoring on purpose. Here
    they are.` (intent filter; ontology В§5.4).
- **Diction.** *Streams 2.4 (signal) + 2.2 (spectrum).* The proof sentences (`THE_VOICE.md` В§6); the
  notation in the dig only.

### ACT 7 вҖ” Recorded *(Record вҖ” continuity)*

- **Moment.** *This run is saved.* The episode written durably; the next sprint's diff now has a valid
  prior.
- **Camera intent.** *Closure that opens forward.* The run becomes part of a living history, not a dead
  log.
- **Who enters.** `Record` / `Accumulate` (ontology В§2.2, В§4.4). The episode + the refactorlog append.
- **Streams in frame.** *Counts* (`run 11`, `14 changes`). *Signals* (`saved`, `green`).
- **Intervention.** At eject only: the provenance-completeness gate (`THE_VOICE.md` В§5) вҖ” *the history
  has one gap.*
- **Outcomes.**
  - **Positive** вҖ” `Saved this run to your history.` вҶ’ episode line:
    `run 11 В· 2 min ago В· 14 changes В· вң“ green` (`THE_VOICE.md` В§13).
  - **Negative** вҖ” couldn't record / the chain is inconsistent: surfaced, not swallowed вҖ” a
    recorded-but-broken episode would corrupt the next diff (ontology В§3 "Record last"), so a failure
    here is loud and plain.
  - **Edge** вҖ” **the provenance gap** (at eject): `Before I freeze this, the history has one gap: run 7
    was never recorded.` вҶ’ **Record it, or freeze with the gap noted.** **Genesis** (first run): no
    prior to thread вҖ” said as a beginning, not an absence.
- **Diction.** *Streams 2.1 (count) + 2.4 (signal).* `Episode` / `LifecycleStore` never on the surface
  (`THE_VOICE.md` В§2.1 вҶ’ `this run` / `the history` / `the trail`).

### ACT 8 вҖ” Where you are *(timeline + ladder)*

- **Moment.** *The run takes its place; here's the distance to cutover.* The schema's whole life as one
  calm shape.
- **Camera intent.** *Motivate by distance.* The streak read as *distance to the finish* вҖ” the most
  motivating framing (`THE_VOICE.md` В§8).
- **Who enters.** The timeline (runs as dots) and the ladder (the single lever).
- **Streams in frame.** *Spectrums* (readiness `7 of 10`; the ladder per-axis). *Counts* (`11 runs`,

### 5.2 By outcome register (the tone that survives across all verbs)

- **Positive.** Plain confidence, proof one dig beneath. End on the next move, never on `done`
  (`THE_VOICE.md` В§4). `Safe to ship.` / `Done вҖ” and I checked it matches.` The master glances; the
  newcomer is reassured; same words.
- **Negative.** *Kind, never dramatic* (`THE_VOICE.md` В§1.6, В§10). The shape is fixed: **what happened
  (concrete) В· why (plain) В· what you can do (active)**. A refusal is `Can't yet вҖ” here's the one
  thing`, never `REFUSED`. A failure hands over a move and names the safe state (`nothing was
  half-written`). The exit code and stack are real вҖ” and in the dig.
- **Edge.** The hardest design and the clearest tell of quality. The **no-op is a designed line**
  (`Nothing to do вҖ” and that's real.`), not an empty screen. The **unset thing is an invitation**, not
  an error. The **unverifiable** is named honestly (`I couldn't read it back вҖ” re-run when reachable`).
  The **at-scale** opens on the *same calm screen* as the 3-change run (`THE_VOICE.md` В§12). The **red
  in history** is named, never hidden.

### 5.3 The always-rules (the eight principles, as a checklist over every string)

Before any line lands, it passes `THE_VOICE.md` В§1 + В§2.2: calm В· one-surface-two-velocities В·
plain-on-top-proof-beneath В· end-on-the-move В· concrete-over-abstract В· kind-never-dramatic В·
honest-to-a-fault В· it's-yours вҖ” and clears the banned list (no drama, no system-shout, no jargon on
top, no leaked internals, no negation-as-headline). If a line fails one, it isn't done.
